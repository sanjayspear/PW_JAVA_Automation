1. Command to generate code:

mvn exec:java -e -D exec.mainClass=com.microsoft.playwright.CLI -D exec.args=codegen "letcode.in"

2. Command to generate pdf of any give website:

mvn exec:java -e -D exec.mainClass=com.microsoft.playwright.CLI -D exec.args="pdf https://amazon.in amazon.pdf"
